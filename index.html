Feature Deep-Dive

Everything a school needs, in one app

Purpose-built views for every role in a student's journey — the administrator running the school, the teacher in the classroom and the parent at home.

Admin & School Management

Administrators get a powerful dashboard to set up and manage the entire institution — classes, subjects, timetables, staff and student rosters.

  • Classes, subjects & subject-group configuration
  • Teacher, student & parent management
  • Timetable builder & substitution handling

Automated Attendance

Teachers mark present/absent per period from a clean class roster. Records sync instantly to a daily class register.

  • Once-daily, twice-daily & per-period modes
  • Monthly overview & live attendance %
  • Instant absence alerts pushed to parents

Secure Gradebook

Parents get a private, read-only view of published exam results — with subject-wise breakdowns, grades and progress bars.

  • Subject-wise marks, grades & percentages
  • Draft & publish workflow for teachers
  • Homework, seminars & exam results in one place

Firebase & Gmail SSO

Robust account protection with Google Sign-In and email/password, backed by Firebase Authentication and role-based access.

  • One-tap Google (Gmail) single sign-on
  • Role-aware routing: admin, teacher & parent
  • Firestore security rules on every read/write
About the Project

Closing the gap between administration, staff and families

Schosphere was incubated by irouni tech to solve one persistent problem in education: communication fragmentation. School setup lived in spreadsheets, attendance in one register, marks in another, and parents were always the last to know.

Our mission is simple — give administrators a single dashboard to manage their institution, give every teacher a fast tool to record what matters, and give every parent a calm, trustworthy window into their child's progress. One platform, updated in real time, accessible to the people who need it.

Admins in full control

Set up classes, subjects, timetables, assign teachers and enrol students — all from one dashboard.

Built for real classrooms

Designed around how teachers actually work — fast rosters, minimal taps, no clutter.

Parents kept in the loop

Instant alerts and clear results mean families are never guessing about school days.
